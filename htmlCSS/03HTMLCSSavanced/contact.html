About Us

Cook Your Soul is a web based magazine connecting art and food. We interview artist/artisans with an aim to bring you culinary & artistic inspiration. We also feature recipes, cartoon, literatures, photographs, paintings, musics, etc in relation with food. We also support emerging and/or independent artists and intend to build a space by giving them opportunities to showcase their work.

Get in Touch

We love hearing from you in the form of comments and e-mails. You can also find us on Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr or Instagram and follow us! Feel free to share your thoughts with us.
