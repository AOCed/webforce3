Eric Bouchet

Eric Bouchet is a french artist based in Paris. Since young, he has painted. He also made short documentaries on environment and fiction films. He’s currently working on a comic book about two captains leaving an adventure on a cargo ship, also a voice over documentary and postcard correspondance art with two other artists.


Interview

I don´t remember my first favorite dish, my parents had have many feasts so I was always surrounded by many delicious things. But the first dish which seduced me is the stuffed eggplant in an afghan style. I was student in an university. The stuffing was mutton meat with sour yogurt. This is the first time I remember I found really delicious in my life. Otherwise, the best culinary memory I have so far was in Portugal. I tasted a kind of fish Pot au feu, not like Bouillabaisse. It’s called Arroz de tamboril. I enjoy fresh broad bean thesedays -april- only with olive oil and some pepper. Ingredient I like to put when cooking is fresh coriander and also good quailty hot pepper.
